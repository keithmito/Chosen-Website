Our Story

Let's delve into the inspiring story of Chosen Generation Sacco, a thriving cooperative born in the vibrant heart of Kiserian. The roots of this remarkable Sacco trace back to November of 2015, when visionary traders at the bustling Kiserian market joined forces. Their mission? To create a financial hub that would empower the community and pave the way for prosperity.

Formally embraced under the Cooperative Societies Act in May of 2017, Chosen Generation Sacco embarked on a journey of growth and impact. Over the past 7 dynamic years, this Sacco has blossomed into a beacon of opportunity, welcoming over 2000 enthusiastic members into its fold by the dawn of 2023.

It's a testament to the spirit of collaboration and resilience that defines Kiserian. Through the dedication of its founders and the trust of its members, Chosen Generation Sacco stands tall as a testament to what a united community can achieve. Becoming a Member

  1. Eligibility: Be 18+, have a national ID, and be able to make sound financial choices.
  2. Membership Fee: A one-time fee of Kshs. 1000.
  3. Open a Savings Account: Start with a minimum deposit of Kshs. 1,050 per month.
  4. Share Capital: Contribute at least Kshs. 2,000 (80 units) to strengthen our Sacco.

The Power of Guarantors

Guarantors (people who vouch for you) provide an extra layer of security for the Sacco. This helps us recover loans in the event of default, protecting our members' investments

The Benefits of Cell Groups

We understand that finding guarantors can sometimes be a challenge. That's why we created cell groups. These groups, based on shared interests or even location, allow members to support each other as guarantors, opening up more loan opportunities.

Your Responsibility
